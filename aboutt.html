Our Workflow

Our booking process involves recording the car details and contents

The technician assesses the vehicle, makes a diagnosis report and the services to be done.

We raise the quotation as per the technician's report and wait for the client's approval.

Upon approval, the technician commences to work on the assigned vehicle

The technical lead conducts inspection and confirms the vehicle is in working order.
